Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

Here's what you need to know: when to salt, what to add, and how hot the pan should be

Fluffy scrambled eggs are a delicious and easy-to-make breakfast or brunch dish. They are made with just a few simple ingredients—eggs, butter, milk, salt, and pepper—but they can be customized to your liking with the addition of cheese, vegetables, or other herbs and spices.

Recipe Details

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 5 mins
  • Active: 5 mins
  • Total: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/8 teaspoon kosher salt (see notes)
  • 2 tablespoons (1 ounce; 30ml) milk (optional)
  • 1/2 tablespoon (1/4 ounce; 8g) unsalted butter
  • Freshly ground black or white pepper

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, beat eggs with salt and milk (if using).
  2. In a nonstick skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat until foamy.
  3. Add eggs and cook, stirring and scrambling gently with a silicone spatula, until large, fluffy curds form and eggs are fully cooked through, about 3 minutes.
  4. Season with pepper and serve.
