Classic French Omelette

Our goal: an enveloping sheet of tender egg cradling a soft-scrambled interior

The classic French omelette is a simple yet elegant dish that has been enjoyed for centuries. It is made with just a few ingredients—eggs, butter, salt, and pepper—but it requires a delicate touch to achieve the perfect texture. The key is to use fresh, high-quality eggs and to cook them over low heat in a nonstick pan. The eggs should be beaten just until they are combined, and then they should be cooked until they are set but still slightly creamy. The omelette should then be folded in half and served immediately.

Recipe Details

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 5 mins
  • Active: 5 mins
  • Total: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1 serving
